Why recycle?

Studies show that deforestation and landfills make up about 25% of the global greenhouse gas emissions annually. These greenhouse gases trap heat in the earth's atmosphere, contributing to climate change. By doing the simple act of recycling, we can each make our impact on saving the planet. The benefits of recycling does not just stop there. Here are more ways recycling positively impacts the world:
  • Reduces amount of waste sent to landfills and incinerators, reducing carbon dioxide production
  • Conserves natural resources like timber, water and minerals
  • Prevents pollution by reducing the need to collect new materials
  • Helps create jobs in recycling and manufacturing
